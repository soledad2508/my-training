### Session Details (HR-based)

| Session Type | Target HR | Purpose |
|--------------|----------|---------|

### Recovery Protocol

**Triggers:**
- RHR ↑ ≥ 5 bpm → reduce intensity  
- Fatigue feeling ≥ 4 → easy day  
- 2+ signals → deload
